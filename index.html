About Us

At AUsome Star Center, we believe that every child is unique and has the potential to thrive in a supportive, inclusive, and nurturing environment. Our Center is dedicated in providing specialized education, therapy, and resources tailored to meet the diverse needs of children with disabilities and learning differences.

SERVICES

AUSOME STAR
SPEECH THERAPY
  • One-on-one and group sessions with licensed speech therapists.
  • Focus on communication skills, articulation, and language development.
OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY
  • Developing fine motor skills and eye-hand coordination.
  • Focus on communication skills, articulation, and language development.
MULTI-LEVEL INTERVENTION
  • Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy.
  • Positive reinforcement, strategies and behavior modification plans.
SPED AND TUTORIAL
  • Personalized learning plans tailored to each student's needs.
  • Regular assessments and progress monitoring.

OUR PROGRAM & FACILITY
At AUsome Star Center, we provide a safe, welcoming, and nurturing environment designed specifically for children with autism. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with sensory-friendly spaces, interactive learning areas, and therapy rooms tailored to meet the unique needs of each child. We believe that every child has the potential to shine, and our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to helping them grow in confidence, communication, and independence. From one-on-one therapy sessions to group activities that build social skills, AUsome Star Center offers a comprehensive range of services designed to support each child's developmental journey. Our facility is not just a place for therapy — it is a place where children are celebrated, families are supported, and progress is made every day. Discover how AUsome Star Center can make a difference for your child and family — because every child deserves to feel like a star.
